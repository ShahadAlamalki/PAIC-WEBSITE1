Taif University - Artificial Intelligence Club

Who We Are

The Artificial Intelligence Club at Taif University is a student organization dedicated to spreading AI awareness and promoting technical knowledge among students. The club aims to empower its members with the understanding of artificial intelligence concepts and their real-world applications through workshops, courses, and interactive projects.

Our Vision

To be a leading student community in technological innovation and in shaping a future driven by artificial intelligence across various fields.

Our Mission

To enable university students to acquire the skills and knowledge needed to understand and apply AI technologies in solving real-world problems, contributing to achieving Saudi Vision 2030 goals in digital transformation and innovation.

Our Goals

  • Raise awareness of artificial intelligence concepts and technologies among students.
  • Organize educational events such as workshops, training sessions, and tech competitions.
  • Encourage creativity and innovation in developing student AI projects.
  • Collaborate with colleges and technical clubs inside and outside the university.
